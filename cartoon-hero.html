Penny Crayon

About Penny Crayon

Pennny Crayon is a 1989 Children's television character; who loves to draw. Living in London; she brings everything she draws using magic crayons to life aiding her on many adventures. The crayons help her get out of tricky situations as well as craeting chaos, until the drawings are erased or simply washed away.



Penny crayon is highly intelligent and resourceful. She has a Northern accent and her favourite hobby is drawing.

Penny Crayon has plenty to aid her on adventures

  1. armed with magic crayons
  2. Highly Intelligent
  3. Can draw on any surface

Penny Crayon doesn't adventure alone

  • She's usually accompanied by her best friend Dennis Pillbeam
  • Dennis is usually praising Penny and building her morale
  • He is also the only regular character throughout the series


Penny Crayon was aired on BBC1 from september 1989 to October 1990

Each episode was approximately 10 minutes
